Our Services

ESCOM Power Supply Permissions

We assist with new electrical connections and approvals from ESCOM including new connections, temporary power supply, additional load approvals, and more.

HT & LT Electrical Installations

Licensed to execute High Tension (HT) and Low Tension (LT) installations including line works, transformer erection, cable laying, and distribution boards.

Transformer Installation Services

Shifting of Electrical Installations

Electrical Office Work & Documentation
